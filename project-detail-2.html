Overview

ITASE 6.0 is an annual national-level Information Technology competition held in Purwokerto. I entered the UX Design category and designed Serenity — a mobile wellbeing app for university students experiencing academic stress. The project covered the full UX design lifecycle, from user research through to a polished interactive prototype.
